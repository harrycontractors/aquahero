Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions? We've got answers! Here are some common queries from our customers.

Do you provide 24/7 emergency plumbing services?

Yes! AquaHero Plumbing provides round-the-clock emergency services, 365 days a year. Plumbing emergencies can happen anytime, and we're always ready to help. Call us at 9127 3131 anytime, day or night!

What areas in Singapore do you serve?

We provide islandwide plumbing services across all areas of Singapore, including Central, East, West, North, and Northeast regions. Whether you're in Orchard, Tampines, Jurong, Woodlands, or Punggol - we've got you covered!

How quickly can you respond to an emergency call?

We aim to respond to all emergency calls within 30-60 minutes, depending on your location and traffic conditions. For urgent situations like burst pipes or major leaks, we prioritize immediate dispatch to minimize damage to your property.

Are your plumbers licensed and insured?

Absolutely! All our plumbers are fully licensed and certified professionals with years of experience. We also carry comprehensive insurance coverage for your peace of mind and protection.

Do you provide free quotes?

Yes, we provide free on-site assessments and quotes. Our plumber will inspect the issue, explain the problem clearly, and provide a transparent quote before starting any work. No hidden fees, no surprises!

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept various payment methods including cash, bank transfer, PayNow, and major credit/debit cards for your convenience. Payment is due upon completion of work unless otherwise arranged.

Do you offer warranty on your work?

Yes! We stand behind our work with a satisfaction guarantee. Warranty periods vary depending on the type of service and materials used. We'll provide specific warranty details with your quote.
